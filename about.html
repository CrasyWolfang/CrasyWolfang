Photography
Wolfang has always had a deeply interest in photography. At the beginning of the 10th decennial he began photography with a simple compact camera. In 2015 he began to join events as an photographer and began to take photographs with a DSLR. Thereafter in 2018 he enrolled in a program for visual production at a technical institute to study photography.

Piano
Piano is a free time hobby. Wolfang began to play the piano in 2011, however the availability to a piano has varied. He is self-taught.Mostly sheet music or MIDI files are used to practice a piece, however, there are pieces that are played by ear.
