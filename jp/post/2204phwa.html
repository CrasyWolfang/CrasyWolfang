The photos below were taken between April and June 2022. In one of the photos taken at Arashiyama there is water visible on the lens. This photo was taken with Nikon's waterproof camera, however, on that day the waterseal stopped working and water leaked into the camera. It's the last picture taken with that camera.

On one of the days the photographer sat in Umeoji Park and with a ukulele. He played peacefully in the park as the creek flowed past him. While he was playing a curious boy came up to him. The photographer showed the boy how to play the ukulele briefly and the boy got to give it a try. He then turned to his mom and told her that he wanted a ukulele, but his mom just sighted in return.
