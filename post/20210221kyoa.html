Kyoto Autumn
Seasonal photography 2021-02-21

The autumn 2020 arrived in Kyoto. It is said that the autumn leaves in Kyoto are especially beautiful. It might be because the old buildings in the Heian city or because the city is surrounded by mountains. Leaves of red and yellow decorated the trees. There were fallen off autumn leaves as well as green leaves still clinging onto their trees.

In Umekoji Park people enjoyed the autumn. Children played by the creek and people relaxed and read books under the autumn leaves as a pair took a walk around the park. Overhead on a branch stood a pigeon and munched on fruits, and on ground-level a small child found a fallen autumn leave that he picked up and closely inspected. Trees that had changed color from top to bottom stood mixed with trees that were still green. If one looked into the foliage of a green tree one could see that even here there was a red leaf that stood out.

The sensation of autumn could also be felt outside of the popular Umekoji Park. By Katsura River a father fished with his 5 year old daughter. In some small park another father tossed a baseball with his son. Along the river people were cultivating their crops and in the Kamo River Park children played on the stepping stones.

A turn towards the mountains and the leaves and acorns where scattered around on the ground. Tree saplings had sprouted from under fallen leaves. In contrast to the strong green ferns they were mixed with dried gray ones. Between the green and brown pine foliage, pine cones still attached to the tree could be seen.
