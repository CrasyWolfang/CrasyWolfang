Eagles and kites
Environment photography 2020-03-05

On this day it began to snow. Eagles were soaring the sky through sunrays and snowfall. On ground a few students flew kites, crows and egrets walked unhurriedly alongside the river and fish swam in the water. There were construction in progress nearby and every now and then an eagle watched the scenery from one of the utility poles.





At night the ambiance changed completely. Besides the occasional car and the ducks in the river it was all silent and the sound of the river could be distinctly heard. The street lights reflected off the water surface between the stepping stones and from time to time the water surface changed color alongside the traffic light.
