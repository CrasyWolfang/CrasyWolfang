Mt. Hiei
Environmental photography 2020-06-22

It was morning. Because it had rained all night a rainbow appeared in front of Mount Hiei as the sun rose.

As one climbs from Otsu towards Mount Hiei the view across Otsu reveals itself, and the further one climb the further the view spans. Eventually the view spans across Otsu, Lake Biwa, Moriyama and further.



Across Mountain Hiei, a driveway opened in 1958 connects Kyoto and Otsu (Encyclopædia Britannica). Besides the driveway all other roads seemed to be hiking trails. There were a lot of temples on the mountain. There were temples with car parking next to the driveway as well as temples deep into the mountain. Along the hiking trails were viewpoints from which the city of Kyoto could be seen in the distance, and if one turned around Lake Biwa could also be seen. If one looks at a map, Mount Hiei separates Kyoto prefecture and Shiga prefecture, which is why it could probably be seen to be a wonderful experience to see both prefectures at the same time.
