Sakura
Seasonal photography 2020-09-04

The Japanese cherry blossom (sakura) is famous. Sakura starts from January in Okinawa and continue northwards through Japan. In the end of March/start of April the cherry flowers bloom in Tokyo and Kyoto, and the flowering continue until Hokkaido.

Philosopher's Road located in Kyoto is famous for sakura. The Kyoto University professor Kitaro Nishida and the Japanese philosopher Hajime Tanabe liked to walk this footpath lined with cherry trees and ponder, which is how it got the name. During sakura season Philosopher's Road is bustling with spectators. However, due to the novel corona virus, besides photographers who come to take pictures of the flowers, people strolling peacefully, and Japanese feeding the pigeons, the road had become void of people. In the silence birds flew from branch to branch and flower petals collected in the flow of the canal.
