Shiga farmer
Environmental photography 2020-06-18

It was a rainy day. Eagles circled the air and crows walked laps on the cultivated fields. A lonely photographer traversed the Shiga country side. That was when the photographer and a farmer came across each other. On the spot the photographer was invited in to see the inside of his greenhouse. Lettuce and other vegetables were planted inside the greenhouse. In the next greenhouse were strawberries.


Afterwards, the farmer showed the greenhouse next to his own house. Tomato saplings and sweet potatoes were prepared inside. Tomatoes are for next season, he explained. Next the farmer took a short interview about farming from the photographer.

Q: "Why did you decide to continue the farm in your parents footsteps?"
A: "I have been doing it since I was young. It is in this system. My family; my wife, my grandmother, grandfather, all have connection to farming. I worked for Panasonic for 42 long years, every since graduation, right. I do this because I like it. Also, it's ought to be good for the health, there is also that. I do it for no more purpose than that of my own desire."

Q: "Do you think your son will continue the farm afterwards?"
A: "My son? No, I wouldn't think so. He has his own life."

Q: "Don't you think it's sad that your son will not continue the farm?"
A: "No, I don't think so. That one does what one wants, that is what it means to live your own life."

After the interview the farmer gave away a rain coat and showed around the neighboring temple. Finally, he took the photographer to the nearby shrine where they parted ways. In the end it had been a encounter of good hospitality.
