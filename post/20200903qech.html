Quiet Echo
Street photography 2020-09-03

Kyoto is famous for being a pretty city. It's close to nature, and there are plenty of temples and shrines within the city. It is said that Kyoto is especially beautiful, and every year a lot of tourists come to see the beauty. Nevertheless, subtle beauty can be found in cities throughout Japan.




In 2020 the novel corona virus "COVID-19" echoed dreadfully throughout the world. In order to limit the infection the Japanese border was closed for almost all foreigners from March 21. Subsequently the a state of emergency was declared on April 07. The lively city sightseeing　scene became almost void of foreigners, and the crowded streets of Kawaramachi changed.

In Namba of Osaka, the next city over, the novel corona virus had the same effect as in Kawaramachi. Shops were still open and similarly the street lights were still on, however, the flow of people had thinned out.





In the end, daily life continued peacefully.
