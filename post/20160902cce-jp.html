Carly Comando「Everyday」
ピアノを弾いてみた 2016年09月02日
曲 Everyday
作曲家 Carly Comando
演奏 狼牙クレシ
2016年09月

Everyday:
The sun shines onto my piano. The piano I've owned for a long time.
The sun rays reveal dust portraying time saying it is untouched but I say nay it is not true.
Dust saying it was deserted but it has never been.
Saying it was abandoned but from my eyes I have never seen.
My piano holds for true and long my every piece, my every soul.
Capsized and fallen over like the days have been revealing something under.
Beneath my skin.
My very heart goes out.
My camera captures. It sees me nervous no doubt.
As for now I lay my hands and puncture time no dust can see.
Filled with emotions almost lost but never forgotten.
Captured within these line of melody.
Stand my piano strong everyday.
Never resign.
Never lay.
And strong I will have you here, by my side as I play everyday.

- Crasy Wolfang, 2016
