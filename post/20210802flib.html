Flowers in bloom
Seasonal photography 2021-08-02

Spring has come and gone, flowers have come to a bloom and now the cherry flower pedals have already fallen.

It was February in the park and all around in the park where flowers in bloom between the dry leaves from autumn and branches. It's the plumb flowers, daffodil and other flowers in bloom. from the sight of these flowers one might understand that spring is on its way.


One week later the feeling of early spring spread out in the park. White-eyes where jumping between plumb flowers, starling wandered underneath the plumb trees and plucked the ground for food, and people visited the park to enjoy the flowers.




In late March the cherry trees were in full bloom. A class of elementary school students had gone to a small road enclosed by cherry trees to play. Within the foliage of the cherry trees bulbuls screeched out into the air, koi where splashed and swam around in the river, and a cormorant spread it's wings on top of a branch that stack out of the river to which a row of children stopped in line to watch the bird. Not only cormorant but also egrets, coots and other animals were along the river shore.



By April the cherry flower had began to fall off and the cherry leaves sprouted. From here the seasons rotated towards summer.
