2025-02-26

Ice

February. It began to snow once again. The snow came to gently pile up around. Even though it's soon March the temperature has dropped. An old man who was encountered on the road to a pond said that "Spring as it may be but it for me is winter-spring. There is more winter than spring." The road eventually led over a small bridge and under that bridge was ice frozen onto the river surface. Although this was nothing more than the simple element of ice, the delicate patterns above caught a pair of eyes, and in it a reminder of how beautiful things exist in this world.

2025-02-10

Archive 2016/2017

Once again the photowall has made it back onto the website. What is the photowall? Well, for a photographer the important thing is to both take photos and to display them somehow. An easy way to do this is to stick printed photos to a billboard in the neighborhood or a wall at work, hence the name, 'photo-wall.' These would commonly be pictures of the activities of that place.

Now, it's easy to upload photos to a website on the internet. Therefore, the leftover photos that couldn't make it into a regular post have the chance to be displayed on the photowall.

Year 2025

It's already 2025. Last year was a busy one. The second impression of Europe has been different from the first one. Therefore time has leaked out into the sand.
