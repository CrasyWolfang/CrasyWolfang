Kites
Environmental photography 2020-03-05

On this day it began to snow. There were kites soaring in the sky through sunrays and snowfall. On the ground a few students flew kites. Crows and egrets walked unhurriedly alongside the river and fish swam in the water. There were constructions in progress nearby and every now and then an eagle gazed over the scenery from one of the utility poles.

At night the ambiance had changed completely. Besides the occasional car and the ducks in the river, the area was silent with only the sound of the river. The street lights reflected off the water surface between the stepping stones and from time to time the water surface changed color alongside the traffic light.
