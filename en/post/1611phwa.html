The Photowall (2016-2)

Mixed photography published: 2025-02-08

It snowed in November, however, by December the snow had already melted away. Furthermore, as new years closed in, an orange stray can found its way into a student's room (where she stayed for a week) which is why photos of her can be found in the album. There have been snowy days this winter, but also rain. In February, after snowfall, the temperature suddenly dropped and ice froze onto the streets. These pictures can be found in this album.
