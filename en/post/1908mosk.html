Moscow

Urban photography
published: 2025-08-25

Moscow. The capital of Russia with a population of 12.62 million in 2019. Moscow has a big soul, does not sleep and its inhabitants don't believe in tears. At least according to songs. Trampling out onto the streets of Moscow you will likely get engulfed in the echo of traffic. Maybe that is the reason why Viktor Tsoi sang that he does not like Moscow. Below are a photographer's snapshots from Moscow.

Note that just like in other big cities, when you close into the popular Red Square there will be a lot of tourists and also some scammers. Scammers will put things (hats, pigeons, etc) on their victims, take a photo and then demand them to pay money. Please be careful of these things when you visit a popular tourist destination.
