Summer '23

Season photography published: 2023-11-08

It's an early July morning of 2023 and the sun rises. The sunlight gradually illuminates the dew-covered grass and the insects wake up. The frog's croaking calls die off and are replaced with the chirps from a coot. Shortly after the coot's chicks come out and swim around in the pond.

The sun rises further and the streets glow up in a golden color. A rabbit still skips down a walking path. When the morning light reaches another pond a duckling comes out and squeaks like a dog toy.

In the forenoon the bees are already out in the park to collect pollen. Ducks and rails swim around in the pond and pigeons walk around by the shoreline. There aren't many people in the park and the birds approach the photographer in hope they'll get some food. A summer day is approaching and if they just wait a little they will surely get food from some other human.
