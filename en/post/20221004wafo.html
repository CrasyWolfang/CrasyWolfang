Waterfowl
Environmental photography 2022-10-22

Outside a city in Sweden there is a pond of blue water. Even the water is clear it is drainage water from the small nearby city. Although there are signs all around that say "Danger! Do not touch the water!" water fowl have built their nests here. Here ducks, coots, swans and cormorants swim and fly around, and sleep by the water line. It is a calm pond separated from the bread eating ducks in the city.
