The beach forest

Forest photography published: 2025-02-26

This trip goes to the forest in Ã„ngelholm. Between the town and the sand beach lies a forest called The Beach Forest (Strandskogen). The forest is a part of the larger forest called Kronoskogen. The south part of the beach is called Sibirien. This is where the U.F.O. monument can be found. North of the monument is quagmire and south is more forest. This part of the forest is far away from the city and therefore there are few people here.

It is popular to swim in the sea here however this far south of Sibirien there are almost no people and in the sand footprints from a horse can be found. At the south end of the beach is a nature reserve, however, there is a river delta which crosses the beach and no way to get over nearby.

The sun set over the sea horizon and the moon climbed up in the east. After the night the morning came and birds could be heard chirping above. A green woodpecker (Picus viridis) also made an appearance this morning. After that the session continued in the town for a little while, and that is all for this trip.
