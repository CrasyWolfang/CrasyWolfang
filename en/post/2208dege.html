This journey is towards Forsakar. Forsakar is a valley in South Sweden. The valley was carved by a river of melted ice during the ice age.ref Today there is a small river that drops down two waterfalls and flows in the middle of the valley. The top waterfall is 7.4 meters and the bottom one is 10.6 meters.ref

North-East of the forest are Degeberga HillsDegeberga backar. On the hills are boulders scattered like in a dream, and around cows graze on the pastures. In the 70's, parts of the film adaptation of Astrid Lindgren's The Brothers Lionheart was recorded here.ref
