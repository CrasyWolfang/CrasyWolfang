CWOLF WEB PROJECT

About

Welcome to the CWOLF WEB PROJECT. This website is a project created and maintained by Crasy Wolfang. The site functions as a hub for produced media like photo albums, self commentary for videos, and more. The projects featured on this site range from school projects and individual creative endeavors to commissioned work and collaboration with other creators.

History

An online presence was first established in 2011 through piano covers and beginner's photography. This activity would later become the foundation for content on the site. The first version of the website was created in 2014 to host pictures of art projects and to share creative video game related projects like video screen recordings, game modifications and piano covers of video game themes. However, the site stayed dormant until 2020 when the project was once again realized and a new version which was better suited for visual projects was hosted. The site was most recently redesigned in 2023 to allow more flexibility in content.
