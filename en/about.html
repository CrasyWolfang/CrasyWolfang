About

This is the CWOLF Web Project. The website is run by Crasy Wolfang. It was created in order to share various content, and furthermore act as a portfolio.

Weblog

Although it is called a weblog, it's not the typical kind of blog that has made it into the modern use. Instead the content of the weblog relates to changes, releases and updates related on the website.

Photography

The photos on the website are of everything from nature to city landscape. The text in posts for photography has been kept low so that the viewer can take their time to look at the photos and enjoy the content. The themes of the photographs range from the feeling and look of place and the beauty of the seasons to portraits of individual people. Both still photos and moving images are used.

Video Production

As for video production, the video is posted together with comments and a self evaluation. On top of description for the video the background and the creative process is also described.

History

Previous creative content has been such as piano covers, paintings, short comics and projects related to video games. Such was posted via other websites since 2011. This website was made public in 2020.
