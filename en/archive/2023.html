Expanded info etc. (2023-11-08)

An information sheet has been added to each post and 6 new photo albums have been published. Three of the albums were taken in spring in the South Ward of Kyoto and follows the Katsura River and the Kamo River as some showcase of Kisshoin. The next three albums were from the following summer in Sweden with one at night. The night album is taken from the night shift worker's perspective for a full year. Furthermore, the photo album category "location" has been divided into 4 new categories. On the top of that "the photowall" which is a mixture of photos have made a reentry on the website.

Unfortunately the amount of photos to review has been immense and portrait photography has been postponed. For now photos of plants and photos of animals have been prepared for revision. Besides photography, a lot of time has gone into software development. Those who know, know about this lengthy project. Since the continuation of the software is sporadic there is no telling of when an alpha version will be available.
